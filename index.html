LYLE

fashion photographer

Lyle Kaiklian is a Manila based, up and coming photographer, born and raised in Cavite, Philippines.
At 18, during his college years in University of Santo Tomas, Lyle bought his first camera and soon developed a keen eye for photography. After college, he worked full-time as a creative director in a start-up IT company. Lyle enrolled himself in School of Fashion and Arts in Makati to further enhance his craft with the help of his mentor, Enzo Mondejar.

"I gravitate towards Fashion Photography. It’s a shared consciousness, a story being told by the team. From the make-up artist’s style, the fashion designer’s choice, and the model’s gesture - every step is paramount in achieving the goal. My best frame is achieved through presenting a glimpse of each artist’s style and personality."
