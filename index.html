SMILE-LESS

“Say Cheese!” can already be considered a catch phrase for anyone taking pictures. It’s omnipresent before the press of the shutter. It’s spoken to evoke happiness in photographs.
As I get deeper with the art and get older in life, I was exposed to different situations and emotions. I learned that a smile can just be a facade. I know that I wanted to present another side of life. Even how ironic it might seem, I wanted to present real situations through my work, through my style.
Hope amidst chaos, beauty in sadness. Smile-less but real.

COLLECTIVE

I gravitate towards Fashion Photography.
It’s a shared consciousness, a story being told by the team. From the make-up artist’s style, the fashion designer’s choice, and the model’s gesture - every step is paramount in achieving the goal. My best frame is achieved through presenting a glimpse of each artist’s style and personality.
